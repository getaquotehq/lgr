Privacy Policy

Last updated 20 July 2026

This policy explains how LEADGEN RENTALS (ABN 83 894 208 171) collects, uses and protects personal information, in line with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

The short version

We collect two kinds of information: details from homeowners who enquire through the campaigns we run, and details from tradespeople (service businesses) who rent our lead assets. We use homeowner details to deliver the enquiry to the single service business who rents that asset. We use service business details to provide, bill and support the rental. We do not sell personal information.

Information we collect

From homeowners (leads)

When a homeowner submits an enquiry form on a campaign we run, we collect their name, phone number, and where relevant their email, postcode and project details, together with their consent to be contacted by the service business named on the form.

From service businesses

When you rent an asset we collect your business name, contact name, email, phone number and the payment details needed to process your rental. Card payments are handled by our payment processor, Stripe; we do not store full card numbers.

How we use it

  • To deliver each homeowner lead to the one service business who rents the relevant asset.
  • To set up, run, bill and support your asset rental.
  • To operate and improve our advertising campaigns and services.
  • To meet our legal and record keeping obligations.

Exclusivity and how leads are shared

Every lead is delivered to one service business only. A homeowner enquiry is passed to the single service business who rents the asset that captured it, and is not sold or shared with any other business.

Disclosure to third parties

We share personal information only with service providers who help us run the business, such as our payment processor and messaging and email providers, and only as needed to provide the service. We may disclose information where required by law.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access, and we retain it only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

Your rights

You can ask us to access or correct the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to delete it where we are not required to keep it. Homeowners can ask the service business who received their enquiry, or us, to stop contacting them at any time.

Cookies

Our sites and campaign pages may use cookies and similar technologies to measure and improve advertising performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Contact

All privacy questions and requests go to contact@leadgenrentals.com.au.

© 2026 Lead Gen Rentals. Lead Gen Rentals runs paid advertising on its own accounts and delivers the resulting leads to a single service business per asset. A lead means a form submission containing a name and an Australian mobile number, captured with named consent to be contacted by that service business. Every lead is delivered to one service business only. Each class carries a guaranteed floor of leads per 30-day period. Where the floor is not met within the period, Lead Gen Rentals will at the service business's election either continue running the campaign at its own cost until the floor is reached, or refund the shortfall pro-rata at the floor rate (period fee ÷ floor). Volume above the floor is typical and varies with market conditions and season; it is not guaranteed. Lead Gen Rentals does not guarantee that any lead will answer, quote or convert. Where a service business holds multiple assets in one area, Lead Gen Rentals maps audiences and creative to minimise overlap and bears any resulting increase in its own advertising costs.
