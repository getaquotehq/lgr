These terms govern the rental of lead generation assets from LEADGEN RENTALS (ABN 83 894 208 171). By renting an asset you agree to them.

1. What you are renting

An asset is a lead generation engine for a specific trade and area: a landing page plus the paid advertising campaigns behind it. When you rent an asset it is locked to you for your billing cycle. We run and fund the advertising on our own accounts. You are renting the output of that engine, not the advertising accounts themselves.

2. What a lead is

A lead means a form submission containing a name and an Australian mobile number, captured with named consent to be contacted by you. Every lead is delivered to one installer only. Where the same mobile number is submitted to more than one of our assets within 30 days, the first submission is delivered and later ones are treated as duplicates and do not count toward any floor.

3. The floor guarantee

Each asset class carries a guaranteed floor of leads per 30 day period. Where the floor is not met within the period, we will, at your election, either continue running the campaign at our own cost until the floor is reached, or refund the shortfall pro rata at the floor rate (period fee divided by floor). Volume above the floor is typical and varies with market conditions and season; it is not guaranteed.

4. No guarantee of outcome

We do not guarantee that any lead will answer, quote or convert. We guarantee exclusivity and the floor, not the behaviour of homeowners.

5. Billing

Rentals are billed month to month in advance through our payment processor, Stripe. Prices are shown exclusive of GST unless stated, and GST is added at checkout where applicable.

6. Cancellation

You may cancel at any time before your next cycle begins. There is no lock in and no exit fee. On cancellation the asset is released and may be rented by another installer.

7. Multiple assets

Where you hold multiple assets in one area, we map audiences and creative to minimise overlap and bear any resulting increase in our own advertising costs.

8. Acceptable use

You agree to contact homeowners only about the enquiry they submitted, to honour any request to stop contact, and to comply with the Spam Act, the Do Not Call Register and the Australian Privacy Principles.
