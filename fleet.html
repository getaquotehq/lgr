Where the floor is not met within the period, Lead Gen Rentals will at the service business's election either continue running the campaign at its own cost until the floor is reached, or refund the shortfall pro-rata at the floor rate (period fee ÷ floor). Where a service business holds multiple assets in one area, Lead Gen Rentals maps audiences and creative to minimise overlap and bears any resulting increase in its own advertising costs.
